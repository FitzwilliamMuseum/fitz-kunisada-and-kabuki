**[Segawa Kikunojo V](Group7.htm)**

Kikunojo V was the son of the actor Segawa Rosaburo, who was a follower of Segawa Kikunojo III (1751-1810). Joining the Kikunojo household, he made his debut with the name Segawa Tamon I, and became Kikunojo V in 1815 at the age of thirteen. That year he also became chief female impersonator (_tate [onnagata](textH.htm)_) at the Kawarazaki theatre. He was a popular, talented and versatile actor, and also wrote several books. He died young at the age of 30.
