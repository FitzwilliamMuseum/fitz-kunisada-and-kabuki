 Dainagon Yorimoto, Kiichi Hogen (Dainagon Yorimoto, Kiichi Hogen)

11/1852

Given by the Friends of the Fitzwilliam with the aid of the MGC Purchase Grant Fund and the National Art Collections Fund.

 

Portrait of Ichikawa Ebizo V (formerly Danjuro VII) as Dainagon Yorimoto, juxtaposed with a poem by Kiichi Hogen, from the series Mitate sanju-rokkasen no uchi (Comparisons with the Thirty-six Immortals of Poetry).
