 Ri (Ri)

06/1856

Given by the Friends of the Fitzwilliam with the aid of the MGC Purchase Grant Fund and the National Art Collections Fund

A posthumous portrait of Onoe Baiju (a name used by Onoe Kikugoro III in retirement and to sign poems) as Rokusaburo the carpenter with a giant carp, from the series Kiyogaki nanatsu iroha (Neatly Written Syllabary in the Seven Styles).
