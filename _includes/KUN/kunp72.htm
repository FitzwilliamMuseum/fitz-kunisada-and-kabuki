 Daiku Rokusa (Rokusa the Carpenter)

Given by the Friends of the Fitzwilliam with the aid of the MGC Purchase Grant Fund and the National Art Collections Fund.



A portrait of Ichikawa Ichizo III as Rokusaburo the carpenter, made for the forthcoming performances of Komamukae Tanomino Tsukkomi (a version of Mijikayo ukina no chirashigaki) at the Morita theatre in 07/1858.
