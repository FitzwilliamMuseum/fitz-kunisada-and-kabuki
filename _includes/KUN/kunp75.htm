 Karigane Bunshichi (Karigane Bunshichi)

10/1858

Given by the Friends of the Fitzwilliam with the aid of the MGC Purchase Grant Fund and the National Art Collections Fund.

A portrait of Katoaka Nizaemon VIII as Karigane Bunshichi, from the series 'Five Chivalrous Men and Women' (Gonin danjo no uchi), juxtaposed with an inset portrait of Ichikawa Dannosuke V as Magebo no Ocho.
